About us

Espressoul Coffee

We are a professional coconut charcoal briquette producer from Indonesia, committed to delivering high-quality products for global markets. Our briquettes are made from 100% natural coconut shell, ensuring clean burning, high heat efficiency, and eco-friendly energy solutions. With strict quality control and modern production standards, we serve customers for shisha/hookah, BBQ, and industrial use across various countries. Our focus is consistency, reliability, and sustainable energy products that meet international standards. This company is built and managed by: Rifqy Fawwaz, Diva Ratu Dwi Saputri, Igad Samudra Together, we are committed to developing a trusted Indonesian charcoal brand that is ready for global competition, focusing on quality, professionalism, and sustainability..
