About us

Espressoul Coffee

My name is Rifqy Fawwaz, and I’m a 20-year-old coffee enthusiast currently exploring the art and culture of coffee. What began as a simple curiosity has grown into a passion for learning about brewing techniques, flavor profiles, and the deeper meaning behind every cup. Espressoul is a reflection of that journey—a space where coffee meets soul, culture, and connection. The name blends “Espresso” and “Soul,” symbolizing energy, depth, and a celebration of life in every sip. Though I’m still learning, my goal is to create a coffee experience that’s warm, inclusive, and meaningful. Espressoul is more than just coffee—it’s a way to share stories, inspire moments, and bring people together.
